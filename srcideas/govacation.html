Go Vacation modding ideas

Improve the gear controls

Especially the hang glider, it's too difficult to turn with that, so I generally avoid that activity

Buddies playing more activities with player

Go Vacation allows you to play with a buddy, which is a computer player that follows you around and will play some activities with you, such as those alrady involving other computer players.

Maybe the buddy AI doesn't feel enough like a sentient being. It copies my every move when following me around, walking the same speed as me, doing the same gestures as me after a second. It plays like all the other COM players. It has to sit out of many of the activities.

What if buddies could best me in all the activites where the person hosting it says "You're gonna have to wait here, %s." to buddies before it starts? What if it gradually got better at the activities? What if it had a conscience?

Export photos in the photo book to PNG or WebP images

I'd like to look at my photo book photos on anything, and maybe share them? But they are only visible in-game, and I don't have a capturing device or want to keep pointing a camera at the screen.

Backport the animal photobook to the Wii version

National Wiiographic Wild

Did the Switch version really need new fonts tho

PC port maybe?

Play on anything that is more powerful and versatile than a Nintendo Wii or a Switch

It can still support Wii remotes through Bluetooth, which may be preferred over a keyboard and mouse depending on how you feel

Camera direction persists after looking

I'm irritated by how the camera jumps back after looking around, can it not?

Cheats and game engine hacking

Shall I summon a marine bike in the mountain resort, and climb up the river? Shall I ride inline skates on sand and snow no problem?

Mark the mail on wheels customers on the minimap ffs

In the Mail on Wheels activity, it would be a whole lot nicer if the customers were marked on the minimap instead of just the game "showing" how to get there

Put real NPC's in the train and trolley

The fake avatars in the trolley that I believe are part of the trolley's 3D model are creepy.

I will program real NPC's to get on and off the trolley.

Avatar editor

You only get to choose from 256 pre-made avatars, 8 categories each having 32. What if I made my own avatar?

Camera adjustments

When moving, the camera is facing a bit too far down, so I can't take in the beauty of the virtual world.

Fix any graphical bugs

Z-fighting in the Track & Soul Hotel, low-poly models being too noticeable in 480p

More Wii MotionPlus

The activities that don't require the Wii MotionPlus don't even use it, but they could really benefit from it, so how about making it optional for those?

Replace the insanely bloated save data format

The save file is OVER TEN MEGABYTES (80 Wii blocks). How in the world did they use that much space? The game would need so much expansion for that to remotely make sense!

Knowing the game and knowing how to make efficient use of space, I could devise a miniscule binary format that will fit snuggly on a floppy disk (1.44 MB), my laptop's BIOS ROM (16 MB), the RAM of my TI-83 Plus (24 KB, 21 KB usable), a hexdump printed on a single sheet of paper (haven't calculated this), the flash of an ATtiny microcontroller (1-32 KiB), and whatever other small data storage you can think of. It should get it down to just one Wii block, smaller than most Wii games that have less to do than Go Vacation.

I began trying this and I just took off PS1 memory card save slot (7.5 KB) because I underestimated this game and don't want to cheat with compression, but my point stands
